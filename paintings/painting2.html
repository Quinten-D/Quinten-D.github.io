GenAI experiment

An experiment with multimodal generative AI assistance. Left is a hand-drawn "pixel art" image, which was given to a multimodal AI model along with the prompt "a baroque still life." The AI model generated an image (right) based on its input which has the necessary details to satisfy the prompt. This proved successful overall, as the model preserved the "spirit" of the initial sketch in its composition and use of color while simultaneously adding the requested amount of detail.
